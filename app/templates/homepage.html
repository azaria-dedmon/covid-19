COVID-19 testing sites

Welcome to the Covid-19 testing website where you can search testing locations in the provided states, sign up for an account, post your test results as well as search the test results of people that you know.

Covid-19 is a highly infectious disease caused by the novel coronavirus. This illness spreads with ease through close contact (within 6ft), exposure to respiratory droplets and has also been declared as airborne. At this point, most of us are knowledgeable on the disease and it's symtpoms, but as a reminder, if you are experiencing: Fever or chills, New loss of smell, Cough or Shortness of breath please seek medical attention immediately. Although this list is not exhaustive of symptoms caused by Covid, it is meant to give a general idea of what to look out for.
